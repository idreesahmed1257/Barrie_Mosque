Making a Difference

  • At Barrie Mosque, volunteering isn't just about giving your time—it's about making a meaningful difference in our community. Whether you're passionate about serving others, organizing events, or supporting our various initiatives, there's a place for you to lend a helping hand.
  • From assisting with community outreach programs to participating in educational workshops and social events, volunteering with us is an opportunity to connect, grow, and positively impact the lives of those around you. Join our team of dedicated volunteers and be part of something truly special at Barrie Mosque.
